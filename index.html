STREETCAR

Escape the heat, hijack the ride, and stack your cash.

Explore Gameplay

The Ultimate Escape

Streetcar is a fast-paced 2D survival game where the city is your playground and the police are your shadow. Master the art of the getaway as you switch between vehicles on the fly, collect massive rewards, and survive the ever-increasing wanted level.

Game Mechanics

Dynamic Wanted Level

The longer you stay alive, the more stars you earn. High stars bring in specialized police units to hunt you down.

Car Hijacking

Not satisfied with your ride? Press Space to jump out and commandeer any vehicle on the highway.

Economy & Shop

Collect money bags or visit roadside shops to buy guns, mods, and new cars to stay ahead of the law.

Diverse Biomes

Race through different environments, from the dense City blocks to specialized zones as you push your survival limits.

Master the Streets

  • WASD / Arrows Move & Steer
  • Space Bar Exit / Enter Vehicle
  • E Key Buy Items (Guns/Cars)
  • F / Click Shoot / Interact
